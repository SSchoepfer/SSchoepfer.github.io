Minimalistic Penrose Tiles

Welcome to our minimalistic website with a subtle Penrose tile background. This design emphasizes simplicity and elegance, providing a clean and focused space for content.

The Penrose tiles are known for their non-periodic tiling, creating a unique pattern that never repeats. We've chosen a subtle grey and white color scheme to keep the focus on the content while maintaining an interesting background.
